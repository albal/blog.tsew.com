Use my special docker image:

docker run -ti -v /FLAC/The\ KLF/Chill\ Out/:/opt albal/cuetools /bin/bash

In the container run:

shnsplit -f Chill\ Out.cue -t %n-%t -o flac Chill\ Out.flac
cuetag Chill\ Out.cue [0-9]*.flac
